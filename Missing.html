Attached is a flyer concerning the disappearance of the 82 year old Garland man, Mr. Hal Ticknor, discussed at the October 30 Retiree Luncheon.  However, Mr. Ticknor has not been verified to have been the one using his credit card in Valley View, Texas, according to later police information that is in conflict with the flyer.  Hal and his wife are members of First United Methodist Church in Garland that has been coordinating a civilian search for him since October 19, after the Silver Alert for him was cancelled due to no other clues being found.  With a full tank of gas his vehicle could travel approximately 500 miles which means he could be anywhere in a very large area.  However, our searches have centered closer to the Valley View, Tioga, Whitesboro area.  

Hal may be asking for directions, but will not remember them.  Please call 911 for any clues or sightings associated with him or his vehicle.  Also, please remember that the vehicle could be a crime scene, so please do not disturb or touch the vehicle.  In the event a person is suspected to be him, please hang on to him until the authorities arrive, or at a minimum, take a picture of him.  Some people have taken pictures and forwarded them to our team if a person they see is thought to be him.  Pictures, questions, or relevant information may be forwarded to Darrell Lancaster, dlanc@verizon.net who will forward the information to the authorities.

At this time we do not have a good idea where Hal will be found or recovered, but are currently searching west of Valley View, Texas.  Anyone wanting additional information about volunteering in the search for Hal may contact Darrell Lancaster via email who will send a volunteer effort description and the coordinator contact information.  Current volunteer efforts are 1) Search and 2) Flyer (electronic or hardcopy) distribution.

Thank each of you so much for your help in finding this person!

Darrell Lancaster
