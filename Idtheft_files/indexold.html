Information Systems Security Alert -
eBay "Phishing" Scam

Thousands of e-mails purportedly from eBay were able to bypass e-mail gateway filters this past weekend. Though the e-mail looks authentic and appears to have come from ebay.com, the source address has indeed been spoofed. The e-mail does not contain any malicious code, such as viruses or Trojans, but is a "phishing" scam, designed to trick recipients into divulging their personal and financial information.

If you received any of these e-mails ignore and delete.
