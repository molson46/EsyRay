Monthly Luncheon for E-Systems/Raytheon Retirees

The informal monthly Retirees Luncheon meeting is held the last Wednesday of each month at the Furr's Fresh Buffet, 1540 Eastgate Drive, Garland, TX 75041,   Most people arrive at 11 AM. Everyone pays his own bill and selects whatever he or she wants to eat. We all sit together in the meeting room.  Attendance usually runs from 40 to 60 people. Spouses are welcome.

After we have finished eating, our meeting leader Dottie Vaughn gives us any news she has on people that have passed away or are ill and how they are doing. Occasionally she passes around get well cards for us to sign. If there is any news from the company she will also pass that on. On occasion we have special speakers from the company and sometimes from local professionals who want to make a presentation that is deemed to be of interest to the majority of retirees. This is always at the end and you are welcome to leave if you are not interested in the particular presentation that month.

During your meal and after the meeting breaks up, you are welcome to visit with old friends and see what they have been doing with themselves in retirement.

A list of luncheon attendees and their home address and email address is normally available at the meeting. You can also sign up to be on the email distribution of the list.
